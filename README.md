# 📚 MedLib: Free Medical Textbook Library

**Empowering medical students worldwide with free, high-quality educational resources**

![MedLib Banner](https://via.placeholder.com/1200x300/0a2942/ffd966?text=MedLib+·+Free+Medical+Textbooks)

## 🌟 About The Project

MedLib is a comprehensive, free medical textbook library created by medical educators for students worldwide. Traditional medical textbooks often cost hundreds of dollars, creating significant barriers for students in developing countries and those with limited financial resources. This project aims to democratize medical education by providing complete, high-quality textbooks completely free of charge.

## ✨ Features

- ✅ **100% Free** – No registration, no paywalls, no hidden fees
- ✅ **Mobile Responsive** – Study on any device
- ✅ **Search Functionality** – Find topics instantly
- ✅ **Category Filtering** – Browse by subject
- ✅ **Interactive Quizzes** – Test your knowledge
- ✅ **Clinical Correlations** – Real-world applications
- ✅ **Memory Aids** – Mnemonics, tables, diagrams
